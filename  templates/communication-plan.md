## 1. Executive Summary
### 1.1 Purpose
_Define the purpose: to establish a comprehensive communication strategy that ensures stakeholders receive timely, relevant, and accurate information throughout the initiative lifecycle._

### 1.2 Communication Objectives
- Ensure stakeholder alignment and engagement
- Maintain transparency and trust
- Facilitate timely decision-making
- Manage expectations and change
- Celebrate successes and milestones
- Address concerns and mitigate resistance

### 1.3 Communication Principles
- **Clarity:** Simple, jargon-free, actionable messages
- **Consistency:** Aligned messaging across all channels
- **Timeliness:** Right information at the right time
- **Relevance:** Tailored to audience needs and interests
- **Two-Way:** Encourage feedback and dialogue
- **Transparency:** Open, honest, and authentic

## 4. Meeting Cadence & Governance
### 4.1 Meeting Schedule
| Meeting | Purpose | Participants | Frequency | Duration | Owner | Agenda / Artifacts |
|---------|---------|--------------|-----------|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Steering Committee | Strategic oversight, decision-making, risk escalation | Executive Sponsor, Product Owner, PM, Architect | Monthly | 1 hour | Project Manager | Status dashboard, risks, decisions |
| Sprint Planning | Plan sprint backlog, commitments | Product Owner, Scrum Master, Dev Team | Every 2 weeks | 2 hours | Scrum Master | Backlog, capacity, sprint goals |
| Daily Standup | Sync progress, blockers, plans | Dev Team, Scrum Master | Daily | 15 min | Scrum Master | Yesterday/today/blockers |
| Sprint Review / Demo | Showcase completed work, gather feedback | Product Owner, stakeholders, team | Every 2 weeks | 1 hour | Product Owner | Demo, acceptance |
| Sprint Retrospective | Reflect, improve processes | Dev Team, Scrum Master | Every 2 weeks | 1 hour | Scrum Master | What went well/didn't, actions |
| Architecture Review | Review design decisions, technical risks | Architects, Tech Leads, Security | Bi-weekly | 1 hour | Solution Architect | Design docs, ADRs |
| QA Sync | Test status, defect triage, release readiness | QA Lead, Dev Lead, PM | Weekly | 30 min | QA Lead | Test reports, defect dashboard |
| Stakeholder Checkpoint | Status update, feedback, alignment | Key stakeholders, PM | Monthly | 30 min | Project Manager | Status report, risks, roadmap |
| Town Hall | Broad updates, Q&A, change management | All stakeholders | Quarterly | 1 hour | Executive Sponsor | Presentation, Q&A |

### 4.2 Meeting Norms
- **Agenda:** Circulated 24 hours in advance
- **Minutes:** Documented and distributed within 24 hours
- **Action Items:** Tracked with owners and due dates
- **Punctuality:** Start and end on time
- **Participation:** Active engagement, cameras on for video calls
