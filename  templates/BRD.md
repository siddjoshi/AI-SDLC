## Validation & Quality Checklist
- [ ] Objectives are SMART and traceable to corporate strategy.
- [ ] All key stakeholders and their expectations are captured with engagement plans.
- [ ] Scope boundaries, assumptions, constraints, and dependencies are explicitly documented.
- [ ] Business process As-Is and To-Be states are described with supporting artefacts.
- [ ] Functional, data, integration, reporting, and compliance requirements are documented and prioritised.
- [ ] KPIs, benefits, and financial analysis include measurable baselines and targets.
- [ ] Risks, issues, and mitigation plans are defined with ownership.
- [ ] Alignment with security, privacy, and regulatory obligations is confirmed.
- [ ] Change management, training, and operational readiness considerations are addressed.
- [ ] Document has been reviewed, approved, and baselined with sign-off recorded above.
