## Validation & Quality Checklist
- [ ] Test scope covers all in-scope functional and non-functional requirements.
- [ ] Test strategy includes levels, types, automation, and defect management.
- [ ] Environments, data, and tooling are documented with ownership and access controls.
- [ ] Roles, responsibilities, and communication plan are agreed with stakeholders.
- [ ] Entry/exit criteria and suspension/resumption conditions are defined for each phase.
- [ ] Test schedule aligns with project milestones and dependencies are tracked.
- [ ] Metrics, reporting mechanisms, and dashboards are specified for transparency.
- [ ] Risks, assumptions, and mitigation plans are documented with owners.
- [ ] Compliance, security, and accessibility testing requirements are addressed.
- [ ] Sign-off from required stakeholders is obtained prior to execution.
