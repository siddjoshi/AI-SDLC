## Validation & Quality Checklist
- [ ] Problem statement is clear, compelling, and quantified with impact of inaction.
- [ ] Strategic alignment to corporate objectives, OKRs, and portfolio roadmap is explicit.
- [ ] Solution options have been evaluated with pros, cons, costs, and timelines documented.
- [ ] Costs are comprehensively estimated with breakdown by category and timeframe.
- [ ] Benefits are quantified, validated, and linked to measurement methods.
- [ ] Financial metrics (ROI, NPV, IRR, payback) are calculated with sensitivity analysis.
- [ ] Risks are identified, assessed, prioritised, and have mitigation/contingency plans.
- [ ] Implementation roadmap includes phases, milestones, resources, and governance.
- [ ] Success metrics, KPIs, and benefits realisation plan are defined with ownership.
- [ ] Stakeholder impact, change management, and adoption strategy are addressed.
- [ ] Compliance, regulatory, and legal considerations are documented.
- [ ] Do-nothing scenario and alternatives are analysed and documented.
- [ ] Document has been reviewed and approved by executive sponsors and portfolio board.
