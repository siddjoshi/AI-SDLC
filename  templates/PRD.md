## Validation & Quality Checklist
- [ ] Product vision and business objectives are clearly defined and measurable.
- [ ] Target personas, journeys, and market analysis are documented and validated.
- [ ] Scope, assumptions, constraints, and dependencies are explicit and agreed.
- [ ] Features and stories include acceptance criteria, NFRs, analytics, and edge cases.
- [ ] UX/UI requirements and accessibility standards are defined with supporting assets.
- [ ] Non-functional, compliance, and localisation requirements are documented.
- [ ] Release strategy, GTM plan, and adoption approach are aligned with stakeholders.
- [ ] Risk register, decisions, and mitigation plans are maintained with ownership.
- [ ] Success metrics and feedback loops are defined for post-launch measurement.
- [ ] Approvals are captured from required stakeholders before development commences.
